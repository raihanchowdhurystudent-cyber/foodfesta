Our Story

A Decade of Culinary Excellence

Founded in 2013 in the heart of Chittagong, FoodFesta began as a small family restaurant with a vision to share authentic Bangladeshi cuisine with the world.

  • Over 10 years of serving authentic Bangladeshi cuisine
  • Recipient of "Best Restaurant in Chittagong" award 2022
  • Sourcing ingredients from local farmers and fishermen
  • Traditional cooking methods with modern presentation
